📖 Understanding the Calculator

This calculator provides accurate weight-based recommendations for fertilizer application. Weight calculations are precise and don't vary by product. Volume estimates (cups, tablespoons) use actual bulk density data and are approximate - they can vary by brand, form, and moisture content.

Why bulk density matters:

• 1 cup of feather meal weighs ~1.7 oz (light and fluffy)
• 1 cup of rock phosphate weighs ~5.6 oz (dense and heavy)
• Same cup, very different weights!

🔢 Fertilizer Numbers Explained

Fertilizer bags show three numbers like 21-0-0 or 10-10-10:

  • First number = Nitrogen (N) percentage
  • Second number = Phosphorus (P₂O₅) percentage
  • Third number = Potassium (K₂O) percentage
